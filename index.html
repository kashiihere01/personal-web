Email copywriting involves crafting persuasive and engaging messages for email marketing campaigns. It's the art and science of writing compelling subject lines, informative and enticing content, and clear calls-to-action to encourage recipients to open, read, and take desired actions.

It involves understanding the target audience, identifying their needs and desires, and using persuasive language and techniques to effectively communicate a message.
